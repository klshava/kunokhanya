Application Process

Step-by-step guide to getting enrolled

1

Gather Required Documents

Prepare your school report/certificate, certified ID/Passport/Asylum document, and 2 passport photos.

2

Submit Application

Visit our office with your documents or submit an online enquiry form. Our secretary will review your application.

3

Application Assessment

Our secretary assesses your application. If successful, you'll be notified to proceed with registration fee payment.

4

Pay Registration Fee

Pay the registration fee via WhatsApp to 082 346 9676. This fee books your place in the course.

5

Enrollment Confirmation

Receive your enrollment confirmation and course start date. You're ready to begin your studies!

Important Information

Registration Fee Payment

The registration fee is paid via WhatsApp to ensure secure, convenient payment. Our secretary will provide payment details upon approval of your application.
