Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Kunokhanya

No, Grade 12 is NOT required. We accept applicants with Grade 9 and above. This makes our courses accessible to more people who want to pursue a career in health sciences and community health.

No upper age limit. We welcome applicants of all ages. Whether you're starting your career or making a career change later in life, Kunokhanya welcomes you.

Yes, weekend classes are available. Part-time classes run on Saturdays and Sundays from 08:00 to 16:00. This allows working professionals to study while maintaining their employment.

Yes, foreign nationals are welcome. Requirements include:

  • English proficiency (minimum conversational level)
  • Valid work/study permit or asylum document
  • Registration fee payment
  • 2 passport photos
  • SAQA evaluation required for NQF Level 4 courses

No on-site accommodation is available. However, the college assists students in arranging nearby lodging options. Contact our office for recommendations and assistance with finding accommodation near our campus.

There is no formal job placement guarantee. However, Kunokhanya has a dedicated job referral office that assists graduates in finding employment opportunities. Our graduates are highly sought after by employers in the health and community health sectors.

Uniforms and stationery are the student's own responsibility. However, we provide guidance on what is required and where to obtain these items. Our office can provide a list of recommended suppliers.

Course duration varies by qualification:

  • Health Promotion Officer (NQF Level 3): 12 months full-time / 18 months part-time
  • Community Health Work (NQF Level 4): 12 months full-time / 18 months part-time
  • Home Based Care Giver (NQF Level 1): 6 months full-time / 9 months part-time
  • Short Programmes: 3-6 weeks depending on the course

We offer flexible payment options:

  • Full payment upfront
  • Monthly installments
  • Quarterly payments
  • Custom arrangements available
  • Study loans through FUNDI and Eduloan

Contact our office to discuss payment options that work for you.

Upon successful completion, you will receive:

  • QCTO Certificate (for QCTO-accredited courses)
  • HWSETA accreditation recognition
  • NQF-aligned qualification
  • Nationally recognized credentials

These qualifications are recognized by employers across South Africa and internationally.
