About Kunokhanya

Kunokhanya Training Academy is a pro-poor institution dedicated to providing quality health sciences education. We believe that lack of fees should not prevent anyone from studying. Since our establishment, we have trained hundreds of community health workers, health promotion officers, and care givers who now serve communities across South Africa.

Located in the heart of Johannesburg CBD, we offer flexible full-time and part-time classes to suit working professionals and students alike.
