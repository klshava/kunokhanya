Flexible, accredited qualifications in health sciences

Main Qualifications

Accredited programs recognized by HWSETA and QCTO

Health Promotion Officer

NQF Level 3

QCTO Certificate

SAQA ID: 94597

Train to educate communities about health promotion, disease prevention, and healthy lifestyles. This qualification prepares you for roles in health promotion, community health, and public health sectors.

Duration: 12 months full-time / 18 months part-time

Schedule: Full-time: Mon-Fri | Part-time: Saturdays

Community Health Work

NQF Level 4

Advanced Qualification

For experienced healthcare workers

Advance your career with this advanced qualification in community health work. Ideal for those seeking supervisory or specialized roles in community health, primary healthcare, and health systems.

Duration: 12 months full-time / 18 months part-time

Schedule: Full-time: Mon-Fri | Part-time: Saturdays

Home Based Care Giver

NQF Level 1

Internal Certificate

Entry-level qualification

Perfect for those starting their healthcare career. Learn essential skills in home-based care, patient support, and community health. No prior qualifications required.

Duration: 6 months full-time / 9 months part-time

Schedule: Full-time: Mon-Fri | Part-time: Saturdays

Occupational Home Based Care Giver

Specialized

Specialized Certificate

Occupational health focus

Specialize in occupational health and safety within home-based care settings. Learn to support clients with occupational health needs and create safe care environments.

Duration: 6 months full-time / 9 months part-time

Schedule: Full-time: Mon-Fri | Part-time: Saturdays

Entry Requirements

General Requirements

  • Grade 9 or above (Grade 12 NOT required)
  • Age 18 or older (no upper age limit)
  • Registration fee payment
  • 2 passport photos
  • Valid ID, passport, or asylum document
  • School report or certificate

For Foreign Nationals

  • English proficiency (minimum conversational level)
  • Valid work/study permit or asylum document
  • Registration fee payment
  • 2 passport photos
  • SAQA evaluation required for NQF Level 4
  • Certified copies of qualifications
